Know About Us

AAROHI is a full service Event Management company that delivers your vision through innovative, sophisticated & fun experiences that connects and inspires. Our talented team of designers and planners pride themselves on delivering to each client nothing less than the highest level of professionalism and attention. At AAROHI planning events are a passion. By only taking on a limited number of clients each year, we ensure the utmost integrity for each event. While we are best known for our weddings and social events, we also have an extensive background in the corporate events world. If you are planning a team building event, product launch or fundraiser, we are here to help!
